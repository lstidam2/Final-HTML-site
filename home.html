On to the books!

Currently Reading:

The Stolen Heir by Holly Black

In this new young adult duology, readers return to the world of Elfhame (The Cruel Prince) to meet Queen Suren eight years after the Battle of the Serpet. Suren lives in the human world, alone in the woods, haunted by the abuse she faced in the Court of Teeth. After a near fatal chase with a monster from her past, Suren finds herself saved by Prince Oak. Suren is wary of the seventeen-year-old prince, whose charm is as deadly as his blade. Suren is faced with the decision to help Prince Oak on his quest in the northern faeryland or to stay in the human world, contining to be haunted by her past.

Just Finished:

The Folk of the Air Trilogy by Holly Black

The Folk of the Air series begins with The Cruel Prince. The young adult trilogy follows Jude, a mortal girl living in the land of faeries, and Prince Cardan, the seemingly wicked crown prince of the land. Jude and Cardan must endure a world of constant poltical battles, betryal and contempt, while navigating their everchanging feelings for each other.

Overall, a fun, short series with lots of action and political espionage. My series rating: 4/5

Next Up:

Fourth Wing by Rebecca Yarros

Fourth Wing is a high fantasy with romance and lots of dragons, right up my ally. The story follows Violet as she strives to become a member of the elite dragon riders and get through Xanden, the ruthless wingerleader of the cadre. I'm looking forward to slow burn of romance as well as the competition between riders. I expect plenty of betrayal and hidden agendas. Everyone on BookTok has been raving about this book since it's May release, so I am excited to see what the hype is about!
